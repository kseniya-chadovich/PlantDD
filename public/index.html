Welcome to Plant Disease Detector!

What is this App?

The Plant Disease Detector app helps farmers and plant enthusiasts identify and diagnose diseases in their plants with just a few clicks. Simply upload a picture of the plant, and the app uses AI to detect the potential diseases.

This tool can help you keep your plants healthy by catching issues early.
