What is this App?

The Plant Disease Detector app is a powerful tool designed to help farmers and plant enthusiasts identify and diagnose diseases in their plants with just a few clicks. Users simply upload a picture of the plant, and the app uses advanced AI technology to analyze the image and detect potential diseases. This quick and easy process eliminates the need for guesswork or time-consuming consultations, making plant care more accessible and efficient for everyone.

This tool is especially important for maintaining the health of staple crops like wheat, which millions of people rely on as an affordable source of nutrition. Early detection of diseases can prevent crop loss, protect yields, and ensure food stability in regions where agricultural success directly impacts food security. Whether used by large-scale farmers or home gardeners, the app provides a convenient way to safeguard plants, reduce losses, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.

Logged-in users can customize their profiles and access the History section, where they save uploaded pictures and AI evaluations. This feature allows them to track historical data about their crops, helping them build insights and make more accurate predictions for future harvests.
